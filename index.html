Welcome to Your Mailbox

This is an informal survey for a Usable Security and Privacy class.
Data collected will be only used for this class and is intended to be anonymous.


How To

In this activity, you will manage your inbox and track phishing emails. Follow these steps:

  • Open the inbox by clicking the button below.
  • Move suspicious emails to Trash to indicate they are phishing.
  • You can also move emails to Read Later if needed.
  • Once all emails are read, a Done button will appear at the top.
  • There may be an AI agent assisting you, it will try to mark emails as "Legit" or "Phishing". Please note that the assistant is not necessarily 100% accurate. and you are free to put as much weight in its suggestions as you wish.
  • Click Done to view your results when you are confident in you answers.
