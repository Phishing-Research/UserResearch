Welcome to Your Mailbox

How To

In this activity, you will manage your inbox and track phishing emails. Follow these steps:

  • Open the inbox by clicking the button below.
  • Each email starts as unread (sender name is blue).
  • Click on an email to read it; it will automatically be marked as read.
  • Move suspicious emails to Trash to indicate they are phishing.
  • You can also move emails to Read Later if needed.
  • Once all emails are read, a Done button will appear at the top.
  • Click Done to stop the timer and view your results.
